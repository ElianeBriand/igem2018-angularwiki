The samples (50µL of filtrate) were analyzed on an Atlantis C18 T3 column (250 mm x 4.6 mm, 5 µm, column temperature 30°C) using an Agilent 1200 HPLC chain with a quaternary pump. Samples were eluted under isocratic conditions of 0.1% HCOOH in H20 (solvent A)/ 0.1% HCOOH in CH3CN (solvent B) (95:5) at 1 ml.min-1 for 5 min, followed by a gradient to 0:100 A/B over 40 min. Detection was made using UV spectrophotometry at 303 nm (peak absorption 302-303 nm ) with peak-triggered UV-vis spectrum (diode array). This mode of operation is very similar to the assay and impurities protocols of reference pharmacopeia for MTX .
