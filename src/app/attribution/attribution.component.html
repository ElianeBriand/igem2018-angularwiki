Attribution

We, at iGEM GO Paris-Saclay are proud to state that we conceived the idea for Methotrexite. We designed and created from top to bottom every single step of the degradation process of the Methotrexate (MTX), implemented it inside Escherichia coli and designed the best model, in order to prove that, GMO and synthetic Biology can solve the biggest problems of society :-)

That being said, we would like to thank our advisors for their help, patient and guidance during this incredible journey.
