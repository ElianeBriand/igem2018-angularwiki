Parisian meetup
The Parisian meetup was the first time we presented our project to all the French teams: Bordeaux, Toulouse, Pasteur, Evry, Grenoble and Sorbonne University. They gave us some amazing feedback about our project and lots of compliments about our presentation. We were blessed to be there, and after this meetup we kept in touch with IGEM Bordeaux, Sorbonne and Grenoble which we were already talking to (more information below).
Sorbonne university
Sorbonne University/IGEM GO Paris_Saclay is probably the best example of what a real collaboration is. Because our team lacked programmers and designers, they proposed to help us with the coding part of IGEM (which really helped us in creating our wiki), and with the sketching process of our new logo. We, on the other hand, realised that there was no mathematician in their team. Our two mathematicians proposed to work with them on the modelling of their project.
